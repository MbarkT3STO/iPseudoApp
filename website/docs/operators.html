‚öôÔ∏è Operator Precedence

Operations are performed in this order (highest to lowest):

  • Parentheses ( )
  • Exponentiation ^
  • Multiplication/Division/Modulus *, /, mod
  • Addition/Subtraction +, -
  • Comparison >, <, ==, etc.
  • Logical not, and, or
Pro Tip: When in doubt, use parentheses to make your intent clear!
